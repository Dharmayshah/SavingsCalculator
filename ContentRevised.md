Design / UX Tips to accompany this copy:

Avoid aggressive words: Never use words like "trap," "hidden fees," "scam," or "fight the banks." Use words like "optimize," "reprice," "collaborate," and "seamless." This keeps banks happy to work with you.

The "Exact Rupee" Promise: Make sure the UI emphasizes that you provide a specific ₹ number, not a range like "Save up to X amount." This is a massive trust builder mentioned in your doc.

Speed Indicators: Use small tags near CTA buttons saying things like (Takes less than 2 minutes) or (Zero physical paperwork required) to eliminate the fear of delays.
